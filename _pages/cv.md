## Research Projects

### Rare-cell-aware Coreset for Single-cell Clustering
2025.9 - 2026.6

- Designed a rare-cell-aware coreset framework for large-scale scRNA-seq clustering, focusing on efficient representation learning and rare-cell preservation in AI4S scenarios.
- Built a multi-view sampling strategy combining graph connectivity, inverse-density weighting, and micro-cluster rebalancing to increase the selection probability of rare, boundary, and transitional cells.
- Used Self-Tuning Gaussian Kernel graph reconstruction and 1-NN label extension to preserve local topology and clustering boundaries after data compression.

### Survey of scRNA-seq Cell Type Annotation Methods
2025.7 - 2025.12

- Conducted a systematic survey of automatic cell type annotation methods, reference databases, and downstream analysis workflows for scRNA-seq data.
- Summarized reference-based, marker-based, clustering-based, traditional ML, DNN/GNN, Transformer, and LLM-based annotation methods.
- Organized more than 70 representative methods by model framework, input dependency, database source, annotation granularity, and application scenario.

### ChatGLM-based Chinese Function Word Usage Prediction
2024.1 - 2024.5

- Built annotation guidelines and datasets for Chinese preposition and conjunction usage prediction, covering 109 prepositions and 93 conjunctions.
- Annotated 10,468 preposition instances and 7,051 conjunction instances.
- Designed an instruction-tuning task with ChatGLM-6B and used P-Tuning for parameter-efficient adaptation.
- Achieved 93.91% accuracy for preposition usage prediction and 94.71% accuracy for conjunction usage prediction on a 1,000-example test set.
